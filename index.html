About

Ahava Coffee is a name derived from the Hebrew word for love, and it symbolizes the commitment of various coffee establishments to foster community and connection. There are several notable Ahava Coffee locations that offer a unique and cozy atmosphere for coffee lovers to enjoy.

Ahava Coffee – Stevensville, Montana: Nestled in Montana's Bitterroot Valley, this coffee shop offers both indoor and outdoor seating. Their menu features a variety of beverages, including drip coffee, lattes, teas, and sandwiches like the 'Tapuach,' a grilled delight with apple butter, ham, havarti, and cream cheese.

Ahava Coffee Co. – Pasco, Washington: With two locations in Pasco, this coffee company emphasizes community by serving specialty coffee, house-made crepes, and pastries. Signature beverages include the 'Canela Oat Milk Latte' and 'Spanish Latte,' crafted with organic ingredients.

Ahava Coffee House – Monroe, Ohio: Located inside the Cincinnati Premium Outlet Mall, this family-owned coffee house partners with Crimson Cup Coffee and Tea to serve premium handcrafted beverages. They pride themselves on using high-quality coffee beans and aim to give back to both local and global communities.

Each Ahava Coffee location embodies the essence of its name by creating spaces where individuals can come together, enjoy quality beverages, and build meaningful connections.
